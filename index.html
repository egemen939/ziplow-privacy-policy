Ziplow – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Ziplow ("we", "our", or "the app") is a media compression application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Ziplow processes all files locally on your device. No files, images, or videos are ever uploaded to any server.

Permissions

  • Storage / Media: To read and save compressed files on your device.
  • Photos / Gallery: To let you browse and select photos and videos for compression. We do not upload or access your gallery without your direct action.

Third-Party Services

Ziplow does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Ziplow does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
